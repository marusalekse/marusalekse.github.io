About Me

Research & Background

I'm a PhD student at the University of Ljubljana working at the intersection of algebra and graph theory, with a focus on understanding how groups act on combinatorial structures.

Research Interests

My work focuses on symmetry in discrete mathematics, exploring how algebraic structures govern the behaviour of graphs and networks.

  • Permutation Groups
  • Groups Acting on Graphs
  • Graph Symmetry
  • Algebraic Combinatorics
  • Vertex-Transitive Graphs
